WHAT IS SOCIAL MEDIA?

Collection of online platforms and apps that allow people to create, share, and interact with content while connecting with others. It enables users to communicate, express ideas, and build communities through posts, images, videos, and messages in real time. Popular examples include Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok, often used for networking, entertainment, and marketing.

Example of Social Media

Here are examples of applications used for communication, online shopping, and awareness campaigns

WhatsApp

“WhatsApp is a free messaging app that allows users to send texts, voice messages, make voice and video calls, and share multimedia files. It is available on both mobile and desktop platforms, making it versatile for personal and professional use.”

Shopee

“Shopee, launched in 2015, is a leading e-commerce platform in Southeast Asia and Taiwan. It offers a user-friendly interface that allows consumers to shop for a wide range of products, including electronics, fashion, home goods, and more, directly from their mobile devices or computers”

Instagram

“Instagram is a visual-centric social media platform with over 2 billion monthly active users globally. It is widely used for building awareness through engaging visuals, interactive stories, and short-form videos like Reels. Its diverse content formats make it a powerful tool for awareness campaigns targeting a broad or niche audience.”
